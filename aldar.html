Al Dar Bakery

Al Dar Bakery and Grocery Market, is an exciting addition to the vibrant Al Shamkhah neighborhood. Designed with the aim of providing residents with access to fresh baked goods and a wide selection of groceries. The project features a modern automatic bakery, a well stocked supermarket, and a curated collection of retail shops, all catering to the everyday needs of the community.

Scheduled for a highly anticipated launch in June 2023, Al Dar Bakery and Grocery Market is positioned to become the go-to destination for residents seeking convenient access to freshly baked goods and a comprehensive range of groceries and essentials.
