About Us

Esaad Middle East Commercial is a UAE company with a solid experience of 20 years in agencies and representations. Its core business is to provide a range of products and services to the UAE Armed Forces.

To source any product or service for which the Armed Forces have requirement.To support that requirement in a professional and cost effective way with a fast reaction times.To be the link between End User and your Business; to appreciate the request through the support of experts and consultants; to provide vital information on the recommented solution.

To provide intelligence and support as a consultancy For the Armed Forces. To be considered as the only firm which can source solutions effectively, reliably and instant reaction times.
