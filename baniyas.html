Baniyas Mall

In the heart of downtown Baniyas East-8, proudly stands Baniyas Mall, as the flagship development in our portfolio. Encompassing an expensive area of over 5000 square meters, this vibrant shopping destination boasts a curated collection of more than 120 specialized outlets, offering a shopping experience catering to fashion, fabrics, jewelry, gold, and accessories.

Since its establishment in 2012, Baniyas Mall has become the ultimate fashion and jewelry destination in the region earing a reputation for its exceptional offerings, providing visitors with an extensive array of choices and unparalleled shopping experiences.
