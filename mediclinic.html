Mediclinic Baniyas

Through our long-standing partnership with the Mediclinic Grou, we developed Mediclinic Baniyas Center. Strategically located in Baniyas East-9, this center offers the community and surrounding areas convenient acces to a Comprehensive range of primary medical services.Patients can benefit from an array of specialized desciplines, including Dermatology, Ear, Nose and Throat (ENT), Family Medicine, Internal Medicine, Obstetrics, & Gynecology, Ophthalmology, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, Radiology, Pathology/Laboratory, and Physiotherapy.

Since its establishment in 2014, the center has become a valued healthcare resource catering to the diverse needs of families in the community.
